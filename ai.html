The Evolution of AI Image Generation

Artificial Intelligence has fundamentally changed how we perceive digital creativity. But where did it all begin? The journey of AI Image Generation dates back to the early days of neural networks, but the real breakthrough happened with the introduction of GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) in 2014 by Ian Goodfellow.

The Rise of Diffusion Models

While GANs were impressive, they were difficult to train. Fast forward to 2021-2022, a new architecture called Diffusion Models took the world by storm. Unlike previous methods, these models work by adding "noise" to an image and then learning to reverse the process, effectively "dreaming" an image out of pure static.
